Food Essentials

  1. Mini Refrigerator

    2. Chances are you will find yourself wanting food at midnight. A mini-refrigerator will provide access to food for 24 hours and make it so you can store leftovers if you go out with your friends!

  2. Plates, Bowls, Cutlery

    3. Even though you will eat at the dining hall almost every day, there will come times you are going to bring food to your dorm room or make food in your dorm. Having access to plates, bowls, and cutlery, you will be able to enjoy meals anywhere you'd like.

  3. Kettle

    4. Kettles are incredibly convenient and an easy way to heat water. You can use the heated water to make hot coffee, tea, soup, oatmeal, and much more.

  4. Keurig

    5. If you are a coffee lover like me, then a Keurig or any coffee appliance is a necessity. Having a Keurig can make it easier to make coffee if you are running late to class or need a boost at 3 a.m. while studying for finals.
