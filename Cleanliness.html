Bath Essentials

  1. Towels and Towel Rack

    2. Hopefully when you go to college you plan to shower. If so, towels and a towel rack are a necessity to dry yourself off and keep your towels dry before their next use.

  2. Shower Shoes

    3. Let's face it, most bathrooms in your dorms will be dirty. That's why it is important to have some pair of shower shoes whether it be flip-flops or slippers. This way you can avoid stepping in something gross or contracting a fungal infection.

  3. Shower Caddy

    4. There are so many things you need for a shower: body wash, shampoo, conditioner, face wash, a loofa, and much more. Having a shower caddy will make your trips to the bathroom easier and more organized.

  4. Cleaning Supplies

    5. In college your life will not always be squeky clean. Having some cleaning supplies like a sponge, a broom, and some cleaning solutions can help keep your space as clean as possible.
