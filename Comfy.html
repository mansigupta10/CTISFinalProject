Bedding Essentials

  1. Bedspread

    2. College consists of numerous late nights, but when you go to sleep, you need the perfect cozy comforter, fitted sheet, pillows, and pillowcases.

  2. An Extra Blanket

    3. Dorm temperatures can be unpredicatable, so bringing an extra blanket would be a good idea in case it is extremely cold one night.

  3. Throw Pillows

    4. Throw pillows make for a little extra comfiness and add a splash of color to your room. They are also an excellent addition to movie nights with your friends.

  4. Mattress Topper

    5. Trust me, the beds that come with the dorm room are as solid as a rock. If you buy a mattress topper, you can make your bed less stiff and more comfy, helping to avoid sleepless nights.

  5. Rug

    6. The worst feeling is getting out of bed and your feet touching the icy, cold floor. A rug can keep you from stepping on cold floors every morning and can even add a pop of color to your dorm room.
