Backpack Essentials

  1. Wallet

    2. Having a small wallet to throw in your bag or purse is essential. Your wallet is the perfect place to carry loose change, cash, your ID, and more. When you want to make a pit stop at a coffee shop, you will be grateful that you have a wallet.

  2. Water Bottle

    3. It is suggested that one must drink at least 64 oz of water a day and more if possible. Having a water bottle that you can carry around can make sure you stay hydrated and energized throughout a long day.

  3. Pepper Spray

    4. There will be a time when you will be walking alone at night, and college campuses are not always the safest. That is why it is essential to have a small bottle of pepper spray in case you are in a dangerous situation.

  4. First Aid Kit

    5. Having a small first-aid kit with a few band-aids, some Neosporin, and some Tylenol will never hurt. It is crucial that you are safe and healthy while at college, and having a first-aid kit will help in case you are injured or in pain.
