About Us

As graduating seniors at the Early College at Guilford, the R.E.M. Sleep & Co. creators are dedicated to transforming each of your college living spaces into something special. Each member is equipped with their own unique talents and hopes to let their personality shine as they start their college journey this coming fall. Now, let's take a closer look at Rachel, Ethan, and Mansi!

Rachel

Even though she's not that great at math, Rachel's budgeting skills will help make sure you don't go too crazy with the dorm decor. She loves DIY-ing and creative solutions to any budgeting problem.

Ethan

With an eye for design and a clean, modern style, Ethan is your go-to guy for all things interior! Aside from design, he also enjoys calligraphy and taking really cool Instagram pictures.

Mansi

Mansi's organizational skills will help you make sure that you aren't leaving any essentials behind! Though her true passions lie in the medical field, she loves design as a hobby and even upcycles clothes in her free time.
