Practical Essentials

  1. Desk Lamp

    2. You will likely be studying for hours at night. A desk lamp is perfect for late-night studying without waking your roommate up with the bright dorm light.

  2. Command Hooks & Strips

  3. Fan

    4. If your dorm does not have an air conditioner, a fan is a necessity. You can stay cool in the hot summers and springs in the comfort of your own dorm.

  4. Power Strip

    5. Your dorm room will not have enough plugs. That is why a power strip would be super helpful for backup or in case you need to plug in 10 things at the same time, although we do not recommend that.
