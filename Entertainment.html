Entertainment Essentials

  1. Amazon Prime Membership

    2. Amazon is the best place to get anything and everything for an affordable price. With Amazon Prime, you can have whatever you need at your college within two days. Amazon Prime even offers a student discount, so it is definitely more bang for your buck.

  2. Netflix, Hulu, or some streaming service

    3. Everyone needs something to wind down after a long day, and many turn to Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, or any form of streaming service. Copping a streaming service can be a great way to de-stress and catch up on your favorite TV shows or movies.

  3. Other Entertainment: Board Games, Cards, etc.

    4. Playing board games and cards with my friends with a few snacks is one of my favorite things to do. When you have free time in college, playing some sit-down games like these can make for a perfect day.
