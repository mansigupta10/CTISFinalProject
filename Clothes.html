Clothing Essentials

  1. Laundry Hamper

    2. When you go to college, you are going to pile up a lot of laundry. With a laundry hamper, you can keep all of your clothes that need a wash in one place.

  2. Hangers

    3. Many dorm rooms come with a closet where you can hang your clothes. Having hangers is essential to organize your closet and keep your clothes from wrinkling.
